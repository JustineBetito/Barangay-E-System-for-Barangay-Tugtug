Barangay Tugtug

Barangay-Staff

  • Law-Abiding: Follows the rules and ordinances set by the Barangay and the local government.

  • Cooperative: Participates in community programs and uses the E-System for official business.

  • Responsible: Maintains cleanliness and order within their own household and immediate area.

  • Informed: Stays updated on Barangay announcements, news, and digital notices.

Resident

  • Service-Oriented: Dedicated to providing fast and efficient assistance to all residents.

  • Tech-Savvy: Skilled in using the E-System to process documents and requests in real time.

  • Accountable: Responsible for managing community records and public funds with transparency.

  • Proactive: Quick to respond to emergencies and community needs within Barangay Tugtug.
